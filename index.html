SOLAR is a group of young men that meet to learn logic and reason to learn how to defend the truth.

Core Values

The Society of Logic and Reason is founded and governed by logic, which is right thinking. All things that SOLAR occupants decide for the club, be it moral or opinionated, shall be dictated by correct reasoning, using logic. That is to say, one cannot be perfectly logical, because logic (correct thinking) is based on God. God is the only one perfectly logical in all he does. And since we are not God, we are not perfectly logical. However, this does not mean we should not try to be logical. God calls us to be like Him. So we should try to be logical in everything we do.

Mission statement

Mature in knowledge and wisdom of logic and the Bible, equipping ourselves to stand up for moral values, uphold a Biblical worldview, and ultimately defend truth.

WHO are we?

We are The Society of Logic and Reason, or SOLAR. We are a group of homeschooled, conservative christian young men co-founded by Lincoln Bontrager, Lucas Miller, Sammy Buck. For the rest of the members, click the Members tab. To become a member you must be invited by a current member, agree with our Terms and Conditions, and the supermajority of SOLAR members must vote for you to join.

WHAT do we do?

We meet to learn logic, reasoning, critical thinking and apologetics, to use for defending the Truth. Our first step is to examine what we believe and why we believe it, follow the evidence where it leads, and equip ourselves for defending the Truth. With the knowledge we gain, we defend our position in articles and videos.
